
# Project Roadmap
## Smart Media Studio Nakuru – Integrated Website & Photo Studio Management System

---

## 1. Roadmap Objective
This roadmap defines a structured, phased approach to designing, developing, testing, and deploying a customer-facing website and a full photo studio management backend system. It emphasizes logical sequencing, clear deliverables, scalability, and maintainability without prescribing timelines.

---

## 2. Phase 1 – Initiation & Governance

### Objectives
- Establish shared understanding of scope, goals, and success criteria
- Define governance and decision-making structures

### Key Activities
- Review and approval of SRS and SDS
- Stakeholder identification
- Definition of success metrics
- Risk identification and mitigation planning
- Communication and escalation framework

### Deliverables
- Approved SRS and SDS
- Project charter
- Stakeholder register
- Risk and governance framework

---

## 3. Phase 2 – UX/UI Design & Brand Implementation

### Objectives
- Translate requirements into a premium, mobile-first experience
- Establish a consistent and accessible brand identity

### Key Activities
- Mobile-first wireframes
- High-fidelity UI mockups
- Tailwind design system using #fcf4eb and #eba536
- Light and dark theme definitions
- Accessibility validation (WCAG 2.1)

### Deliverables
- Approved wireframes and mockups
- UI/UX style guide
- Reusable Tailwind components
- Accessibility compliance checklist

---

## 4. Phase 3 – System Architecture & Foundation

### Objectives
- Establish a secure, scalable technical foundation

### Key Activities
- ASP.NET Core Razor Pages setup
- Modular project structure
- MySQL schema and EF Core mappings
- Authentication and RBAC
- CI/CD and environment configuration

### Deliverables
- Solution skeleton
- Database ERD
- Authentication and authorization framework
- Deployment-ready environments

---

## 5. Phase 4 – Public Website & Content Modules

### Objectives
- Deliver a visually compelling, SEO-optimized public website

### Key Modules
- Home page with 3D-inspired hero section
- Services catalogue
- Photo and video galleries
- Blogs and articles
- Testimonials with moderation
- Staff profiles
- Contact, inquiries, and live chat

### Deliverables
- Fully functional public website
- SEO metadata and sitemap
- Optimized media delivery
- Accessibility-compliant pages

---

## 6. Phase 5 – Customer Self-Service Portal

### Objectives
- Enable customers to independently interact with the studio

### Key Features
- Customer registration and profiles
- Online service ordering
- Quotation viewing and approval
- Job status tracking
- Invoices and receipts
- Messaging and feedback

### Deliverables
- Customer dashboard
- Secure self-service workflows
- Customer audit logs

---

## 7. Phase 6 – Backend Operations & Administration

### Objectives
- Digitize and streamline internal studio operations

### Key Modules
- Customer management (online and walking)
- Order and job lifecycle management
- Staff assignment
- Media and portfolio management
- Quotations and invoicing
- Reports and analytics
- System configuration and audits

### Deliverables
- Admin and staff dashboards
- End-to-end workflows
- Reporting module

---

## 8. Phase 7 – Online Payments Integration

### Objectives
- Enable secure and traceable payments

### Key Features
- M-Pesa payments
- Card payments (Visa, Mastercard)
- Payment callbacks and reconciliation
- Payment history and reports

### Deliverables
- Integrated payment workflows
- Payment audit reports
- Security validation

---

## 9. Phase 8 – Testing, QA & Hardening

### Objectives
- Ensure reliability, security, and usability

### Testing Scope
- Unit and integration testing
- User acceptance testing
- Accessibility testing
- Performance and security testing

### Deliverables
- QA reports
- Resolved defects
- UAT approval

---

## 10. Phase 9 – Deployment & Go-Live

### Objectives
- Launch the system into production

### Key Activities
- Production deployment
- SSL and domain configuration
- Monitoring and logging setup

### Deliverables
- Live production system
- Deployment documentation
- Monitoring dashboards

---

## 11. Phase 10 – Training & Operational Handover

### Objectives
- Ensure smooth adoption and sustainability

### Key Activities
- Staff and admin training
- User manuals and guides
- Support handover

### Deliverables
- Training materials
- Documentation
- Support transition plan

---

## 12. Roadmap Value
This roadmap delivers a premium digital presence, a complete studio management backend, integrated online payments, customer self-service, and a scalable foundation for national growth.
